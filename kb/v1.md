## Claims

**Q**: Can you help me file a claim with my insurance
**A**: Absolutely. We guide you through the entire claims process, from documentation to meeting with your adjuster, to make sure everything goes smoothly.

**Q**: Do I need to be present during the adjuster appointment
**A**: Yes. Adjusters would never provide claim information without your consent. But one of our team members can meet the adjuster on-site and represent your best interest.

**Q**: My insurance denied my claim - can you still help
**A**: Yes. Our Claims Specialists can review the denial and help file a supplement or dispute if necessary. We’ve helped many customers get approvals after an initial denial.

**Q**: Will you meet with my insurance adjuster
**A**: Yes, we can coordinate directly with your adjuster and attend the inspection to ensure nothing is missed.

## General Roofing Concerns

**Q**: How do I know if I need a new roof
**A**: If you’re seeing missing shingles, leaks, or interior stains - or your roof is over 15 years old, it might be time. We can confirm everything during your inspection.

**Q**: Do you offer financing
**A**: Yes! We have financing options available. A member of our team can explain the options during your inspection.

**Q**: How long does a roof installation take
**A**: Most roofs are installed in one day, depending on the size and weather. We’ll keep you updated throughout the entire process.

**Q**: Will my landscaping or property be protected during the install
**A**: Absolutely. We use tarps and protective equipment to safeguard your home, driveway, and landscaping throughout the build.

**Q**: Can I book an inspection if I’m not the homeowner
**A**: We typically schedule inspections with the homeowner or someone authorized to make decisions about the property. If that’s not you, I’ll collect your info and have a team member reach out to confirm before moving forward.

**Q**: Can I book a specific time for my inspection
**A**: I’ll take note of your preferred time, but exact scheduling depends on availability in your area. A member of our team will contact you shortly to confirm the next available window.
